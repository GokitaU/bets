Basketball bets information

Possible outcomes of a meeting:

1   – the first contestant wins;
X (0)   – the match ends in a draw;
2   – the second contestant wins;
1X (10) – the first contestant wins, or the match ends in a draw;
X2 (02) – the second contestant wins, or the match ends in a draw.

Winner 1 X 2 1X X2
E.g.: Žalgiris - Lietuvos Rytas 1,8 15 2,1 1,6 1,8

In this case the first contestant is Žalgiris, and the second is Lietuvos Rytas. If a member wants to place a bet on Žalgiris winning in regulation time, he has to choose variant 1, and if he thinks that the winner will be Žalgiris or the match will end in a draw (in this case, it is not important who will win the match after overtime, if played) then he has to choose  variant 1X. If it is assumed that the match will end in a draw in regulation time,  variant X should be selected. If one thinks that Lietuvos Rytas will win in regulation time, he/she should choose  variant 2; and if he/she thinks that Lietuvos Rytas will win in regulation time, or the match will end in a draw, he/she should choose variant X2.

Possible bets on the outcomes after the regulation time:

  1. Winner;
  2. Handicap;
  3. Number of points scored by both teams;
  4. number of points scored by a team;
  5. Winning interval;
  6. Number of points scored during the match (even, odd).

Possible bets on the outcomes in regulation time and overtime:

  1. Which team will score more assists;
  2. Which team will be more accurate in making free throws;
  3. Which team will be more accurate in making 3 pointers;
  4. Which team will be more accurate in making field goals;
  5. Which team will score more rebounds;
  6. Number of 3 pointers earned by a team;
  7. Number of assists by a team;
  8. Number of rebounds made by a player;
  9. Number of points scored by a player;
  10. The highest scoring player;
  11. Number of rebounds made by a player.

Handicap - types of bets where special conditions are set for declaring the winner of an event for betting purposes.

E.g.: Pranašumas. 1 X 2 1X X2
/0-10/ LITHUANIA-LATVIA 1,85 15,00 1,85 1,65 1,65

/0-10/ means that the first team (Lithuania) is given a handicap of 10 points, i.e. 10 points are added to the score of the second team (Latvia).

According to the variants:

1   – Lithuania wins the match by 11 or more points;
X   – Lithuania wins the match by 10 points exactly;
2   – Lithuania wins the match by 9 points at most, or the match ends in a draw, or Latvia wins by any number of points;
1X – Lithuania wins the match by 10 points or more;
X2 – Lithuania wins the match by less than 10 points, or the match ends in a draw, or Latvia wins by any number of points.

her example: Handicap. 1 X 2 1X X2
/18-0/ SWEDEN-SPAIN 1,85 15,00 1,85 1,65 1,65

/18-0/ - means that the second team (Spain) is given a handicap of 18 points. In other words, if a person thinks that Spain will win by more than 18 points, he/she should choose variant 2, and if he/she thinks that Spain will win by 18 points exactly, he/she should choose the variant 0. if he/she thinks that Spain will win by 17 points or less, or the match will end in a draw, or Sweden will win by any number of points, then he/she should choose variant 1.

Basketball bets also include the number of points scored by both teams, points scored by specific team, points scored by specific players, and other statistics.

Other example: number of points scored by both teams. 1 X 2 1X X2
/-156+/ LITHUANIA-LATVIA 1,85 15,00 1,85 1,65 1,65

In this case, the company offers betting that Lithuania and Latvia will score in total 156 points..

According to the variants:
1   – Lithuanian and Latvia will score less than 156 points;
X   – 156 points exactly will be scored;
2   – more than 156 points will be scored;
1X – 156 points or less will be scored;
X2 – 156 points or more will be scored.

Cancellation of basketball bets.

Bets may be cancelled in the following situations:

  1. a match was not completed;
  2. when a match is cancelled, or a match did not start on time and it is not clear before 10 a.m. of the next business day whether the match will start within 74 hours from the original time specified by the event organizers;
  3. when a bet is made on obviously “bad” odds;
  4. when the company mistakenly accepts a bet from a player.

when less than 90 percent of the regulation time of the match is played  (i.e. less than 36 minutes), the company will cancel the bets that can change in the event that the match is not suspended (e.g.: which team will score more rebounds, which team will score more assists, number of points scored by a player, which team will be more accurate in making free throws, which team will be more accurate in making field goals, which team will be more accurate in making 3 pointers). Only bets made on the winner are not cancelled provided the winner is announced no later than within 72 hours from the moment the match was suspended.
